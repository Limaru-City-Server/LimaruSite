Federal Republic of Limaru

General Server Rules



  1. No Griefing

    Griefing is strictly prohibited and carries a maximum penalty of a permanent ban.


  2. No Advertising

    Mentioning the IP address, communication channels and/or the social media accounts of non-partner servers is not allowed. However, you may still mention other servers or casually chat about them.


  3. Do not block trains

    Unless given special permission by the railroad company, standing on tracks will result in a fine.


  4. Do not steal or PvP

    Theft and PvP is stricty prohibited and will result in a fine, and the confiscation of your inventory and ender chest.


  5. Graffiti art and street trash is allowed

    In order to spice up our server, we have permitted everyone to build on the side of empty walls, and on streets. However, such art is not allowed inside tunnels or railroad property, unless the owner of said tunnel or railroad says otherwise.


  6. No doxxing

    Do not disseminate any private information about any player, not limited to but including the real name, email address and pictures of faces. Limaru values internet privacy and will not hesitate to take legal action if the privacy of individuals are infringed.


  7. No DM protesting

    Do not message the lawmakers of Limaru to change a law. Parliament exists for a reason.


  8. Respect human rights

    All players are human like you. Do not infringe their rights.


  9. Do not aid in crime

    It is an offence to aid someone else in crime.


  10. Do not wage war on Limaru, or as Limaru

    Waging war against Limaru, either in a roleplay sense or by attacking the server, or waging war against other servers in Limaru's name will get you punished.

Building-Specific Rules



  1. Do not use //fast

    As per the Faster WorldEdit Act 2019, the WorldEdit command //fast is not permitted for any rank. This is to ensure proper CoreProtect logging.


  2. Complete the exterior of your builds

    To keep our server clean, abandoned and incomplete builds will be removed by the Architects after 1 month of inactivity.


  3. Follow local building laws.

    Certain parts of the server may have special building laws, e.g. style restrictions, building type restrictions etc. Please follow these local laws.
